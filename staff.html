Meet the Team

Mulky Prabhakar Adesh - Executive Director

Chef Mulky Prabhakar Adesh is the executive chef at The Green Box. With a career that spans above 3 decades Chef Adesh has travelled across the world cooking a wide variety of cuisines in some of the top restaurants. He has also worked with a healthcare specialty institution, “Madhavbaug Ayurvedic Cardiac Hospital”, where he specialized in Heart Care and Diabetic food.

Why Choose Us

SManaging your health is not just about cutting calories, but also ensuring that you get maximum nutrients form every grain that you consume. That is why every meal is designed to be well balanced and has a count of carbohydrates, fats, protein and the number of calories. Our meals our delivered in convenient microwaveable disposable containers, so that you get hot meals whenever you consume it. The aim of our company is to help people improve their lives and lifestyles through eating our healthy meals. The best customer service and a top quality product are critical to our success. For this reason we choose the highest quality ingredients available, our own chefs prepare them according to strict standards in our own kitchen, and we deliver them to your door in a temperature-controlled environment. We invite you, too, to feel the difference. Treat your health and fitness to a Green Box Meal Plan!
